Hello!

I'm a grad student at Duke University studying computational material science. I am passionate about combining web development and materials science in order to bring to life rich data visualizations for web and mobile platforms.

Web development for the materials science community

In the materials science community, it is of paramount importance that data is made freely available and distributed in a method that can be easily accessible. As a result, web applications provide the perfect medium to accomplish this goal.

They make it possible to navigate large sets of data with ease while also providing tools for data visualization. This all helps in guiding researchers intuition which in the end expedites materials discovery.
